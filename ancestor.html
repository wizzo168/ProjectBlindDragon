Ancestor

Ancient descendants of powerful spirits, Ancestors possess deep connections to the natural world and the memories of those who came before them. Their long lives and mystical heritage grant them unusual abilities and wisdom beyond their years.

Race Traits

Trait Details
Ability Score Increase Wisdom +2, Constitution +1
Age Typically live over 200 years, spending much of that time in infancy.
Size Medium
Speed 30 feet
Darkvision 60 feet
Languages Common

Darkvision

You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You cannot discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Hidden Step

As a bonus action, you can magically turn invisible until the start of your next turn or until you attack, make a damage roll, or force another creature to make a saving throw.

Once you use this trait, you cannot use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Ancestral Magic

The magic of your people still echoes in your shared memories.

Level Spell
1st Dancing Lights
3rd Speak with Animals (1/Long Rest)
5th Locate Animals or Plants (1/Long Rest)

Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

Survivor

You gain proficiency in the Survival skill.
