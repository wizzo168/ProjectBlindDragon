Albinauric

Artificial beings created by mortal hands. Though not born naturally, Albinaurics possess intelligence, emotion, and ambition equal to any other people of the Lands Between.

Race Traits

Trait Description
Ability Score Increase Your Dexterity score increases by 2.
Age Albinaurics are created already mature. They age more slowly than humans.
Size You are Medium.
Magical Creation You have resistance to force damage.
Languages You can speak, read, and write Common.

First-Generation Albinauric

The first generation of Albinaurics were flawed creations whose legs gradually weakened over time. Many compensate through exceptional skill with bows and mounted combat.

Feature Description
Ability Score Increase Your Wisdom score increases by 1.
Speed Your walking speed is 20 feet.
Tamer You gain proficiency in Animal Handling.
Mounted Combatant You gain the Mounted Combatant feat.
Albinauric Ranged Training You gain proficiency with shortbows, longbows, and greatbows.

Second-Generation Albinauric

Later generations of Albinaurics were more physically stable and capable of remarkable agility and martial prowess.

Feature Description
Ability Score Increase Your Constitution score increases by 1.
Speed Your walking speed is 30 feet.
Acrobatic You gain proficiency in Acrobatics.
Cartwheel When you take the Dodge action, your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of your turn.
Albinauric Melee Training You gain proficiency with clubs, greatclubs, and halberds.
