Omen

Omen are feared and reviled throughout the Lands Between. Marked by twisted horns and cursed blood, they are often persecuted from birth. Despite this treatment, many Omen possess incredible physical strength and an innate connection to primal powers.

Base Race Traits

Trait Details
Ability Score Increase Strength +2
Age Mature at a similar rate to humans.
Size Medium (typically 7–8 feet tall).
Speed 30 feet
Creature Type Fiend
Languages Common

Omen Blood

When you fail a saving throw against Bleed, each creature within 5 feet of you must make a Dexterity saving throw.

On a failed save, the creature takes 1d10 fire damage.

The save DC equals:

  • 8 + your Charisma modifier + your proficiency bonus

Reviled

Omen are feared and distrusted throughout society.

  • Advantage on Intimidation checks against humanoids.
  • Disadvantage on Persuasion checks against humanoids.

Horned Omen

Your horns were never removed. Though heavily ostracized, your curse manifests more strongly than that of most Omen.

Trait Details
Ability Score Increase Charisma +1
Cursed Blood Melee weapons you wield that do not inflict Bleed instead inflict Bleed 1.

Maimed Omen

You survived the brutal removal of your horns and learned to endure hardship through discipline and martial training.

Trait Details
Ability Score Increase Constitution +1
Combat Training Proficiency with light armor, medium armor, and heavy weapons.
