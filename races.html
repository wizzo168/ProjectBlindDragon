Races

The Lands Between are home to many strange peoples and creations. Whether born of divine lineage, crafted by mortal hands, or descended from ancient spirits, your race shapes your place in the world.

Choose one of the races below when creating your Tarnished.

Race Selection Guide

Your race determines your ancestry, physical capabilities, unique traits, and cultural background.

Review each race carefully before selecting your Tarnished's origin.
