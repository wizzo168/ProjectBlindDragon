Races

The Lands Between are home to many strange peoples and creations. Whether born of divine lineage, crafted by mortal hands, or descended from ancient spirits, your species shapes your place in the world.

Choose one of the races below when creating your Tarnished.

Available Races

Race Description
Albinauric Artificial beings created through mortal experimentation.
Ancestor Spiritual descendants tied to the ancestral spirits.
Demi Human Tribal peoples possessing great ferocity and adaptability.
Living Jar Animated vessels filled with the remains of mighty warriors.
Numen Rare people said to originate from lands beyond the Lands Between.
Omen Individuals born bearing powerful primordial curses.

Choosing a Race

Your race grants unique abilities, traits, and roleplaying opportunities. Each race represents a different origin within the Lands Between and influences how your Tarnished interacts with the world.

Review each race carefully before making your selection.
