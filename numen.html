Numen

Mysterious and exceedingly rare, the Numen are said to have come from lands beyond the Lands Between. Their ancient bloodlines carry forgotten knowledge and a natural affinity for magic and history. Though few in number, their influence can be found throughout the world's most significant events.

Race Traits

Trait Details
Ability Score Increase Intelligence +2, Charisma +1
Age Reach maturity near the end of their first century and rarely die of old age.
Size Medium
Speed 30 feet
Darkvision 60 feet
Languages Common and Undercommon

Darkvision

You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You cannot discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Ancient Ancestry

The ancient blood of your people grants unusual resilience against magical influence.

  • You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed.
  • Magic cannot put you to sleep.

Ancient Lore

You are exceptionally well-studied in the stories and mysteries of old.

  • You gain proficiency in the History skill.
  • You can cast Identify at will without expending a spell slot.
  • Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell.
